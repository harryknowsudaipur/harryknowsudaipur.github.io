About me

Hi, I'm Harry

I grew up right here on the lake, and I've spent years since then guiding people through this city — the palaces everyone photographs, the food stalls most people walk straight past, and the quiet corners that make Udaipur feel like home rather than a checklist.

No script, no franchise, no handoffs between five different people in a day. Just me, a car, and a genuine love for this place — hoping every guest leaves feeling like they actually got to know the city, not just its postcards.

The deal

No agency, no middlemen, no "let me check with my manager"
